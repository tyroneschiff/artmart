How it works

Three steps from
crayon to canvas.

No scanner, no design skills, no fuss. Just point your camera and let the magic happen.

1

Photograph
the drawing

Point your phone camera at any drawing your child makes — crayon, marker, watercolour, paint. It takes seconds.

2

AI elevates
the artwork

Our AI reads the drawing and transforms it into a gallery-quality piece — watercolour, oil, or digital art — keeping the soul of what your child made.

3

Share, vote
and print

Each child gets their own store. Share the link with grandparents and family. They can vote on favourites and order a print shipped to their door.
