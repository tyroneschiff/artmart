# Acquisition — Rejected & Decided Against

*(Maintained alongside CLAUDE.md `## Acquisition strategy`. Cap: 15 items. Trim least-relevant when full.)*

- **Meta / TikTok paid ads** — At ~$42 LTV minus 30% Apple fee minus ~$11 COGS, max sustainable CAC is ~$15. Achievable CAC in kid lifestyle category is $25–60. Math does not work. Revisit only if LTV crosses $80.
- **Google Search Ads** — No search intent for "AI art from my kid's drawing" yet. Buying generic "kid art app" intent gets the wrong user. Skip until category awareness exists.
- **TechCrunch / press launches** — Wrong audience. Tech press readers are not the parent ICP. A single TikTok by a mom influencer beats any TechCrunch headline.
- **Product Hunt launch** — Same reason. Audience is builders, not parents.
- **Cash payments to influencers** — Parenting influencers post about products their kids love organically. Free credits + a kind note converts better than paid sponsorships in this category and does not require FTC disclosure that lowers trust.
- **Subscription-first positioning** — Already rejected in CLAUDE.md `## What we've tried and rejected`. Credit packs win until usage data justifies a tier.
- **Generic "preserve your child's art" headline** — Buries the magic. Lead with delight ("step inside your child's drawing"), preservation is the side effect. Per CLAUDE.md `## What this is`.
